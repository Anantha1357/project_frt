How mobile phone are Devil to us ?

In 2012, a survey was conducted to study mobile phone effect on society by identifying how it has impacted people’s lives on an individual level. The participants were provided six options that included both negative and positive impacts. The results of the survey revealed that most of the people think that mobile phones have positively impacted their lives. While there are definitely some negative effects of cell phones, they can be avoided if users are careful and use their phones in moderation.
